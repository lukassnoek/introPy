PsychoPy¶

In this week, we will cover the essentials of PsychoPy. The lecture will give an introduction to stimulus presentation. The following tutorials wills give an introduction to the PsychoPy software package (Introduction to Psychopy), discuss its Builder interface (Introduction to the Builder and A Builder experiment from scratch), and its Coder interface (Introduction to the Coder and A Coder experiment from scratch).
